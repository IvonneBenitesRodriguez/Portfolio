About this project

Casa Yllika is a student's house where international students live in Peru. Our client desired to offer its students a tourist guide information about the useful areas to visit like supermarkets, banks, hospitals, universities, and between others places in the district where the student's house is placed.

  • Our objective is create a detailed and personalized information to international students with useful tips of living in our local area.
  • Students can access it from any device.
  • It has a navigation bar | Homepage | categories: Important Facts * Useful tips * In case you need places |
    and useful links.
  • This website is SEO Friendly.
  • This website is built using BOOTSTRAP, CSS3 and HTML5.
